About Boot Up Program

Connecting college students to the ecosystem of contineous innovation, enriching their skillset and paving the pathway to be industry ready!

The Bootup program consists of a bunch of hackable templates for various kinds of activities which will help the participants to learn, implement and share the knowledge of being industry ready in different fields

Before you race off to the boilerplates, here are few community good practices to keep in mind

  • Lead by Example
  • Be Respectful
  • Watch your language
  • Take responsibility for our words & actions
  • Expect and accommodate cultural and personal differences (read more here)

The Bootup kits

The templates are open and hackable , these are merely the blueprints of the points the organizer can derive from while planning from any event. Templates are subjected to alteration depending on the target audience.

  • IoT on Fedora Robotics Suite
  • Mobile App Dev
  • Containerziation and Virtualization
  • Web Development with Flask
  • Introduction to FOSS and Fedora
